How to Join the Realm

  1. Open Minecraft and choose Realms.
  2. Find Block Party Realm after your invite is approved.
  3. Ask a parent first before requesting access.
  4. Join, build, explore, and be excellent to each other.

Request a Realm Invite

Invite Only

Want to Join?

Block Party Realm is a private family-and-friends Minecraft Realm. To request an invite, ask a parent first and do not share personal information.

The request button opens an email. Only include your Minecraft username, how you know someone from the Realm, and whether you play Java or Bedrock.

Request Realm Invite

Do not post your full name, phone number, address, age, school, or private information.

Server Rules

  • Be kind. No bullying, griefing, or mean chat.
  • Ask before using someone else’s build or items.
  • Keep names, skins, signs, and builds family friendly.
  • Help new players. Everyone starts somewhere.
  • Have fun, build big, and use your imagination.
